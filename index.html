Spaghetti

spaghetti
    Ingredients
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Tomato sauce
  • Noodle of choice
    Steps
  1. grab a large and small pot
  2. fill up large pot halfway with water and place on stovetop, set high
  3. pour tomato sauce in small pot and set burner to medium-low, stir occasionally
  4. Once the water comes to a boil toss in a few tbs of salt and put in noodles
  5. Set timer for 8 minutes, stir every few minutes
